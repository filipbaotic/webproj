About

Pavone Art Gallery is home to many world-known historical and contemporary artists. It's the place where everyone can discover and experience modern and contemporary visual art and design. We explore complex topics of yesterday, today and tomorrow. This vision is guided by a fresh, energetic approach to displaying, caring for and renewing our world-famous collection.

We build memories for the future.
